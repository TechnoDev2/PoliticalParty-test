Overview

The People's Protection Party seeks to ensure that the well-being of the people is not only for the benefit of the people but also for the well-being of other living entities.

I want the people of a political party to remain trustworthy and dependable in the face of any obstacles and impediments, to hold on to the power of any party, revenge, every cause, hatred, jealousy, greed, lust, temptation, addiction, arrogance, and the courageous effort of the people in my mind.

Selfishness, deceit, contempt, insults, slander, are not present in this party. I feel fortunate to be one of you in giving priority to the youth as opposed to politics like the last rotten intrigues in our current democracy. Furthemore, It is my firm determination to serve you as your person while looking after myself in all of you ... I come forward to introduce myself to you all.
